About Me

As a board-certified Behavior Analyst, my background is in applied behavior analysis. I am honored to have worked with many children with disabilities and their families. Designing evidence-based, highly individualized treatment plans for each client with measurable criteria for success ensures a smooth learning process, increasing the quality of life for all involved. I bring my expertise in the scientific principles of behavior to my work in UX Research. Behavioral observation, data collection, interviewing, user testing, and data synthesis/analysis are at the forefront of my skills. I also have a keen eye for UI Design and style. I am passionate about inclusive, user-centered design. My dream is to play a part in making technology more accessible for all humans.
